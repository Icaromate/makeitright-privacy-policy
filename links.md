## Join the Beta Test

To download the game, you need to join the tester group first so your email gets added to the Play Store access list.

**Step 1:** Join the Google Group

[Click here to join the tester group](https://groups.google.com/g/make-it-right-closed-testers)

**Step 2:** Download the game on the Play Store

[Click here to download Make It Right](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.ianmcbubbles.makeitright)
